# About Philanthropic Playlist

We are a student-led organization at the University of Illinois that brings students together through music, performance, and service. We are built around the idea that music can do more than entertain — it can create community, raise awareness, and support meaningful causes.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to use music as a way to connect students with charitable causes. Through rehearsals, performances, and fundraising efforts, we aim to build a welcoming community while supporting local organizations and initiatives that make a positive impact.

## What We Do

We meet weekly to rehearse musical selections chosen by our members. These rehearsals lead to an end-of-semester performance, where we use music as a platform to support fundraising and community outreach.

In addition to performing, members can help with event planning, fundraising, outreach, playlist curation, and organization leadership.

## Who Can Join

Philanthropic Playlist is open to students of all instruments and experience levels. Whether you have years of performance experience, are returning to music after time away, or simply want to be part of a music-centered community, you are welcome here.

You do not have to be a music major to join. Anyone interested in music, philanthropy, fundraising, or community-building can get involved.

## Why Music?

Music has a unique ability to bring people together. It gives students a shared creative space while also creating opportunities to support causes beyond campus. Philanthropic Playlist uses that connection to turn performance into impact.

## Get Involved

Interested in joining, performing, helping with events, or learning more? Connect with us through our GroupMe, newsletter, or Instagram.
