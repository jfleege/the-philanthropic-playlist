# Philanthropic Playlist

**Music, Community, Giving**

Philanthropic Playlist is a student organization at the University of Illinois focused on bringing people together through music while supporting meaningful causes on campus and beyond.

## Who We Are

We are a student-led music and philanthropy organization open to musicians of all instruments and experience levels.

## What We Do

We meet weekly to rehearse member-voted musical selections, culminating in an end-of-semester performance that supports our fundraising efforts for charitable causes.

## Why It Matters

Our goal is to use music as a way to build community, raise awareness, and support local organizations doing important work.

## Get Involved

Whether you are interested in music, fundraising, performing, or just meeting new people, Philanthropic Playlist is open to students at the Univeristy of Illinois who want to make an impact.
